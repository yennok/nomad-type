About Nomad Type

Nomad Type is an independent type foundry exploring multilingual design across Hebrew, Arabic, and Latin scripts. We create typefaces that balance cultural authenticity with contemporary use, bridging writing systems through research and craft.

Our work is driven by the belief that type design should respect the unique characteristics of each script while creating harmonious relationships between them. We approach multilingual typography not as a technical challenge, but as an opportunity to explore the rich cultural and historical contexts that shape how we communicate.

Each typeface we develop undergoes extensive research into the traditions and contemporary practices of the scripts involved. We work closely with native speakers and cultural experts to ensure our designs maintain authenticity while meeting the demands of modern typography.

From display fonts that make bold statements to text faces designed for extended reading, our collection reflects the diverse needs of today's multilingual communication landscape.
