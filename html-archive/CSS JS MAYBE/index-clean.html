The design process for Safra involved extensive research into the visual characteristics of Arabic, Latin, and Hebrew scripts. Each script has its own rhythm, proportions, and aesthetic qualities that needed to be harmonized while maintaining their individual character. The result is a typeface that feels native to all three writing systems, creating a seamless reading experience across different languages and cultures.
